About Bed Nest

Where comfort meets community in the heart of downtown

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate travelers, Bed Nest was born from the belief that budget accommodation shouldn't mean compromising on comfort, cleanliness, or community.

After years of traveling the world and staying in countless hostels, we knew exactly what travelers needed: a modern, clean space with premium amenities at an affordable price. We wanted to create more than just a place to sleep – we wanted to build a community where solo travelers, backpackers, and digital nomads could connect, share experiences, and feel at home.

Today, Bed Nest stands as a testament to that vision. Located in the vibrant heart of downtown, we've welcomed over 15,000 guests from more than 80 countries, each bringing their unique story to our community.
