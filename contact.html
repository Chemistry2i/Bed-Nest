Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What time can I check in?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM to 11:00 PM. For late arrivals after 11 PM, please contact us in advance to arrange special check-in.

Is breakfast included?

Yes! We provide a complimentary continental breakfast daily from 7:00 AM to 10:00 AM, including coffee, tea, cereal, bread, and fruits.

Do you have luggage storage?

Yes, we offer free luggage storage for guests before check-in and after check-out. Secure lockers are available 24/7.

Is there a curfew?

No curfew, but we ask guests to respect quiet hours from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM. The building is accessible 24/7 with your room key.

Can I book for groups?

Absolutely! We welcome groups of 8 or more. Contact us directly for group rates and to ensure we can accommodate your entire party.

What's your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your arrival date. Same-day cancellations are charged 50% of the first night's rate.
